"The Mountain Spoke" Adventure Tours

Gallatin Valley

The Gallatin Valley guided tour is a begginer level adventure that is fun for all ages. It is just over 7 miles to a park destination making the entire trip around 15 miles. This takes most groups around 5 hours (not counting a 1.5 hour lunch stop).

Bike rental: Adult $120 // Youth $80
**Price per person**
  • $22 - Group of one.
  • $20 - Group of two and three.
  • $18 - Group of four and five.
  • $16 - Group of six or more.
  • $14 - Group of ten or more.
June 28 - August 25 // Monday - Saturday
**Approximate Time**
  • 09:00 - Departure from parking lot.
  • 11:30 - Arrival at park.
  • 11:30 - Lunch (provided).
  • 13:00 - Departure from park.
  • 15:30 - Arrival at parking lot.

Saddle Peak

The Saddle Peak guided tour is a full day adventure that is recommended for intermediate riders. It is just over 15 miles to a mountian peak making the entire trip around 31 miles. This takes most groups around 10 hours (not counting a 1 hour lunch stop).

Bike rental: Adult $120 // Youth $80
**Price per person**
  • $80 - Group of one.
  • $75 - Group of two and three.
  • $70 - Group of four and five.
  • $65 - Group of six or more.
  • $60 - Group of ten or more.
June 28 - August 25 // Monday - Saturday
**Approximate Time**
  • 07:00 - Departure from parking lot.
  • 12:00 - Arrival at mountain peak.
  • 12:00 - Lunch (provided).
  • 01:00 - Departure from mountain peak.
  • 06:00 - Arrival at parking lot.
